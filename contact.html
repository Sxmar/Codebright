Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects may take 6-8 weeks or longer.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and running smoothly.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for all devices, from smartphones to desktop computers.

Can I update the website myself?

To make changes, you will need a working knowledge of programming. Without direct access to and understanding of the underlying code, manual updates will not be possible.

Do you provide hosting services?

While we don't provide hosting directly, we can recommend reliable hosting providers and assist with setup and configuration.

What if I need changes after launch?

We offer ongoing support and are always available to make updates or improvements to your website as your business evolves.
